About Amity University

Our History

Established in 2005 by the Ritnand Balved Education Foundation (RBEF), Amity University has grown to become India's leading private research university. Founded by Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan with a vision to revolutionize higher education, Amity started with just 120 students and has since expanded to over 150,000 students across 12 campuses worldwide.

Key milestones include:

  • 2005: Established as the first private university in Uttar Pradesh
  • 2010: Achieved NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation
  • 2014: Launched international campuses in London, Dubai and Singapore
  • 2020: Recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India

Mission & Vision

Mission: "To provide value-based education and nurture future leaders through quality teaching, innovative research, and global exposure while contributing to societal development."

Vision: "To be a global education leader recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation that transforms lives and benefits society."

Our Values

Amity University is guided by core values that shape our culture and approach to education:

  • Excellence - Commitment to the highest standards in academics, research, and infrastructure
  • Innovation - Fostering creativity and cutting-edge research across disciplines
  • Integrity - Maintaining ethical standards and transparency in all operations
  • Global Perspective - Preparing students for international careers through global collaborations
  • Social Responsibility - Developing programs that address societal challenges

Leadership

Under the guidance of our Founder President, Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan, and Chancellor, Dr. Atul Chauhan, Amity has established itself as a center of academic excellence. Our leadership team combines academic vision with professional expertise to deliver world-class education.
